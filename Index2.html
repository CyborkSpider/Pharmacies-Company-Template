Our History

PharmaOverseas: A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1995 by the visionary Dr. Ahmed Hassan Hafez
Gazzarine, may he rest in peace, PharmaOverseas was
established with a clear professional and commercial vision.
Built on a foundation of continuous development, the company has consistently strived for excellence.

In 2004, a strategic partnership was formed with CERP, a
leading French drug distribution company, where
PharmaOverseas acquired a 20% stake. Subsequently, in
2014, the company transitioned into an Egyptian joint-stock
company

PharmaOverseas plays a pivotal role in the supply chain,
Focusing on two critical aspects:

-Storage of Pharmaceutical and healthcare products
-Distribution of pharmaceutical and healthcare products
